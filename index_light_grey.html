FAQ

Quick answers for the “wait… how does this work?” questions.

Do I have to share my Tesla password?

No. You authenticate through Tesla’s authorization flow. We never ask for (or see) your Tesla password.

Can a scammer generate a report for a car they don’t have?

To generate a Tesla-connected report, someone must authorize access from a Tesla account that has access to the vehicle. Owner-level verification is shown when available.

Will the report show my home address or exact location?

By default, we avoid sensitive personal details like exact location. You choose what to share with buyers.

Is this the same as Carfax?

No — OUT Check is a Tesla-connected report focused on verifying access and summarizing vehicle details buyers ask about (warranty, options, alerts, snapshots). It does not replace title/accident history services.

How long is the report valid?

Reports are time-stamped. Many sellers regenerate right before a serious buyer visit so the snapshot is fresh.
